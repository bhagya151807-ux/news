About InsightStream

InsightStream is a college project built to demonstrate the power of web technologies in presenting information. The platform allows users to navigate the news landscape through a simple and clean interface.

Our goal is to help readers explore news in different categories, search for specific topics, and stay informed with trending updates.

This project is made using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It focuses on clear design, smooth user experience, and functional features.
